Ohio

Ohio, also known as the Buckeye State, is a North American state located in the Midwest region of the country. It was admitted as the 17th state on March 1st, 1803 and now has a population of 11,756,058 residents. It is located below Lake Erie and is bordered by West Virginia, Kentucky, Michigan, Indiana and Pennsylvania. Originally, the capital of the state was Zanesville and Chillicothe, but was changed to Columbus in 1816 (Knepper, 2023).
