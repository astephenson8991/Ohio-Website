Cleveland, Ohio

This urban city is located on the edge of Lake Erie in North-Eastern Ohio. The home of Rock and Roll was incorporated in the year 1810 and became a central point for boats' transportation of various goods. Today, the economy is still largely influence by it's heavy industry manufacturing (Britannica, 2023).
