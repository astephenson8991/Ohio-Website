Toledo, Ohio

At the North-Western corner of the state, you can find Toledo. This urban area was incorporated in 1837, but was greatly disputed with Michigan as to which state owned the land. Deposits of natural gas gave way to the citys' success as did its position near the Maumee River and Lake Erie (Britannica, 2023).
