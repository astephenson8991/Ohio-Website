Columbus, Ohio

The capital, Columbus, is an urban city located in central Ohio. It was incorporated in 1834, but became the state capital in 1816. Because of the Erie canal and an 1850 railroad, the development of this area boomed as a hub for transportation and commerce (Britannica, 2023).
